Education

Use these resources and learn how to get the help you need.

Cancer in Children — https://www.cancer.org/
American Cancer Society (ACS) provides a wealth of information key to understanding and fighting against cancer.
How the American Cancer Society Fights Childhood Cancer — https://www.cancer.org/
Articles pertaining to ACS's approach towards childhood cancer.
Leukemia in Children — https://www.cancer.org/
Information regarding leukemia-type cancer in children.
Children and Families — https://www.lls.org/
Leukemia & Lymphoma Society's vast list of childhood cancer resources.
Resources for Pediatric Oncology Patients and their Families — https://www.lifewithcancer.org/
INOVA's list of resources pertaining to childhood cancer.
Definition of Childhood Cancer — https://www.cancer.gov/
National Health Institute's (NCI's) official definition of childhood cancer.
