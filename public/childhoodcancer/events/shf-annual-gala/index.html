SHF ANNUAL GALA CAMP QUALITY U.P. KIDS KICKING CANCER

October 1st, 2022

Save the date!

The Superior Health Foundation Annual Gala is scheduled for Saturday, October 1, 2022, in the Grand Ballroom of Northern Michigan University in Marquette, Michigan. With COVID-19 and the worldwide pandemic, the event is subject to change. In the event a live gathering cannot be held, SHF will host a live virtual event.

For 2022, SHF will partner with a health-centered organization and award $15,000 in funding to that organization. We’re proud to announce that their partner is us! Its funding will be used to help with travel and medical costs for families who need to travel for care; support Camp Quality Upper Peninsula, a camp for pediatric patients and their families at Bay Cliff Health Camp; and support Kids Kicking Cancer, a non-profit that empowers children to manage pain and stress through martial arts and meditation.

Sponsorships are available. Learn more here.
